About SpaceX Dragon

Dragon,also known as Dragon 1 or Cargo Dragon,was a class of partially resuable cargo spacecraft which was developed by SpaceX,an american private space transportation company.The Dragon spacecraft is capable of carrying up to 7 passaengers to and from the Earth orbit,and beyond.

Interior design of Spacex Dragon

Engine Darco

  • The Dragon spacecraft is equipped with 16 Draco thrusters used to orient the spacecraft during the mission,including apogee maneuvers,orbit adjustment and attitude control.Draco thruster is capable of generating 90 pounds of force in the vacuum of space.

Getting to Orbit

  • The Crew Dragon lifts off from Florida's Kennedy Space center on a version of spacex Falcon 9 rocket that has been adopted for astronauts.In the event of an emergency on the pad or during the climb to orbit,the launch escape system will fire to propel the capsule and its crew away from the rocket.

Development Funding

In 2014,Spacex released the total combined development costs for both the Falcon 9 launch vehicle. NASA provided US$ 396 million while SpaceX provided over US$450 million to fund both development efforts.
